## Tips for Writing Blog Posts

1. **Title**: Keep it catchy but clear
2. **Excerpt**: 1-2 sentences that hook the reader
3. **Category**: Pick the one that fits best
4. **Featured**: Only one post should be featured at a time
5. **Published**: Toggle this ON when you're ready to share
